Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! We ship worldwide from Kyoto. Shipping times vary: Japan 2–4 days, Asia 5–10 days, Europe/USA 10–18 days. We use eco-friendly packaging and ship with Japan Post and DHL depending on destination.

Absolutely! Custom crochet plushies, personalised art prints, and commissioned ceramics are all possible. Use the contact form above with "custom" in the subject line, and describe your dream piece in as much detail as you like. Custom orders typically take 4–8 weeks.

All crochet items come with a care card. Generally: gentle hand-wash in cool water, no wringing, reshape and air dry flat. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight to preserve the colours. With gentle care your plushie friend will last many, many years.

Because every item is handmade and unique, we can't accept returns unless an item arrives damaged. If anything arrives in less than perfect condition, please photograph it and contact us within 7 days — we will always make it right. Custom orders are non-refundable once work has begun.

Our crochet plushies use safety eyes and non-toxic fibres, but they are designed as collectibles for ages 3 and up. Ceramics and art prints are for adults and older children only. If you're unsure, just ask — we're happy to advise on which pieces are best for little ones.
